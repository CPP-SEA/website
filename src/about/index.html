About

Mission

The Software Engineering Association (SEA) teaches and encourages the professional skills needed to be a Software Engineer, such as code review, unit testing, communication, software design, and more. Our online and real life exercises allow anyone, novice or professional, to sharpen and practice their Software Engineering skills. Research and project opportunities will build on these learned skills.
